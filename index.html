  • Utilized by the 2nd largest public agency of the City of New York to promote bicyclist saftey

Pizzabot

Pizzabot is an autonomous food delivery and a vending robot that is inspired by the hundreds of pizzerias in New York City. Its primary purpose is to deliver pizza from local pizzerias to customers in an urban environment. Additionally, just like a New York City pizzeria, Pizzabot can carry up to 8 individual slices of pizza that can be sold to anyone who happens to walk by or see the robotâ€™s location on an app. The integrated touchscreen display allows a user to interact with the robot and easily purchase a slice. Pizzabot also accepts contactless cards and cash so any New Yorker can enjoy a spontaneous slice of pizza. The rugged terrain wheels and LiDAR sensor enable Pizzabot to navigate complex urban environments without human assistance. The Pizzabot also has a built-in heating system to keep pizza warm for longer deliveries.

  • NYC FIRST 2021 Build Beyond CAD Challenge Winner
